## [0.2.0] - 2020-11-30

### Changed

* Changed `Module::text_offset` from `u32` to `usize`.

### Removed

Breaking changes:

  * `stream_name_unicode`
  * `doc_string_unicode`
  * `cookie`

Non-breaking changes:

* `Information`
  * `doc_string_unicode`
  * `help_file_2`
  * `constants_unicode`
* `ReferenceProject`
  * `name.1`
* `ReferenceRegistered`
  * `name.1`
* `ReferenceOriginal`

* `ReferenceControl`

  * `name_extended.1`
